The studio

If a show needs a safe pair of hands, we're already plugged in — vision, streams, comms and cue lights, run by people who've done it for thirty years.

Founded in March 2020 by James Jones and Ben O'Dwyer, Virtual Studio Events started by streaming an awards show to 75,000 people when the world shut down. Today the same crew engineers the technical layer of live, hybrid and broadcast events for the UK's leading production companies, agencies, charities and brands.
